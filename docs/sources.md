# Data Sources for Chandigarh Highway Disasters

This document tracks the potential and utilized data sources for the CHD-HDD project.

## 1. Traffic & Accident Data
- **iRAD (Integrated Road Accident Database):** [irad.parivahan.gov.in](https://irad.parivahan.gov.in/)
  - *Data Type:* Detailed accident reports, locations, and causes.
- **Chandigarh Police Traffic Statistics:** [chandigarhpolice.gov.in](https://chandigarhpolice.gov.in/content/crime-and-traffic-accident-statistics)
  - *Data Type:* Annual/monthly accident counts, blackspot mapping.
- **NCRB (National Crime Records Bureau):** [ncrb.gov.in](https://ncrb.gov.in/)
  - *Data Type:* ADSI reports for macro-level traffic death statistics.
- **MoRTH (Ministry of Road Transport and Highways):** [morth.nic.in](https://morth.nic.in/)
  - *Data Type:* Annual national reports with state/city breakdowns.

## 2. Weather & Environmental Data (For Road Icing/Fog)
- **IMD Chandigarh:** [mausam.imd.gov.in/chandigarh/](https://mausam.imd.gov.in/chandigarh/)
  - *Data Type:* Visibility, temperature, and precipitation data (crucial for fog/icing correlation).
- **Open Government Data (OGD) Platform:** [data.gov.in](https://data.gov.in/)
  - *Data Type:* Historical weather and traffic datasets.

## 3. Geographic Data
- **Chandigarh Smart City Portal:** [chandigarhsmartcity.in](https://chandigarhsmartcity.in/)
  - *Data Type:* Road network GIS data, urban planning maps.
- **OpenStreetMap (OSM):** Can be used to extract highway geometries for Chandigarh (NH-5, NH-44, etc.).

## 4. News & Media (For Real-time/Unreported Incidents)
- **The Tribune / Hindustan Times (Chandigarh Editions):** Useful for gathering qualitative data on specific disaster events.
